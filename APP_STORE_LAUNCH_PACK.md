This document keeps FlowType's App Store positioning honest and consistent with the current product.

## Positioning

- Product promise: `Speak once. Send polished.`
- Category fit: Productivity
- Core value: Turn rough speech into send-ready writing on iPhone.

## App Store Metadata

- App Name: `FlowType`
- Subtitle: `Turn speech into polished writing`
- Promotional Text: `Record a thought, review the result, and turn it into clear writing for email, Slack, notes, and task lists.`
- Keywords: `dictation,voice notes,transcription,writing,productivity,email,notes,slack`

## Description

FlowType helps you turn rough voice notes into clean, send-ready writing.

Speak naturally, then review polished text before you copy or share it anywhere on your iPhone. FlowType is built for busy workdays when you need to capture a thought quickly and turn it into something clear.

Use FlowType to:

- turn meeting follow-ups into polished messages
- clean up Slack updates before sending
- draft emails from speech instead of typing
- capture task lists and notes while you are on the move
- quickly rewrite text to be shorter, friendlier, more professional, or formatted as bullets

Why people use FlowType:

- fast capture without typing first
- review before sending
- simple copy and share flow
- recent drafts saved on device

FlowType only records when you tap Start Dictation. Your voice is processed securely in the cloud to create polished text.

Use this in the App Review notes field when submitting:

`FlowType records audio only after the user taps Start Dictation. Anonymous authentication is created automatically for app functionality. Users can delete their anonymous account in Help and Status > Delete Anonymous Account.`

## Claims To Avoid

Do not claim any of the following until the product truly supports them:

- custom keyboard
- system-wide typing replacement
- works inside every app without copy/share
- offline transcription
- no cloud processing
